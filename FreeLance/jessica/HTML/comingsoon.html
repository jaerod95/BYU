Jack Fey

by Jessica Draper

Cricket Reese, magician-in-training, "A" student, and all around good girl is wondering what to do with her graduate work...and her life...now that the Master Magician she was planning on studying under was devoured by demons.

Enter Jack Fey. Business man. Find thing, get thing, bring thing...at least that's the "plan." Jack and his business partner, Meleon, find themselves in dire need of a magician. What happens next...well, three worlds may not live to tell the tale!
