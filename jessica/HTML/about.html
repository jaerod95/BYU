About Us

New worlds, smart characters, fascinating stories - fiction as you've never seen it before!

We're Fate Luck Karma Publishing, your source for imaginative and unusual fiction. If you're looking for engaging characters, well-developed worlds, interesting ideas, and exciting plots you've come to the right place. With no foul language, gratuitous sex, and over-the-top violence, you can enjoy the stories without the "cringe" factor. FLK is the place for stories, ideas, and characters you won't find anywhere else.

FLK Books' offerings include urban fantasy, last days, LDS fiction, high fantasy (often with a twist), futuristic cyber adventures, alternate reality, and pretty well any other genre, subgenre, or non-genre that appeals to us.
